    • A Journey into Parisian History

    For centuries, the Seine riverbanks were at the heart of Paris's economic and cultural life.
    Since the Middle Ages, they have been a vital hub for river commerce,
    hosting bustling markets and boats. Today, these banks are a UNESCO World Heritage site,
    highlighting their historical significance.
    Exploring these areas, you will encounter iconic bridges such as the Pont des Arts or Pont Neuf,
    each with a rich history and fascinating anecdotes.
    Temporary exhibitions and festivals frequently animate the banks,
    offering visitors an immersion in contemporary culture while celebrating Paris's glorious past.

    Discover Paris from the Seine with a riverboat cruise.

    Enjoy a family or friends picnic on the landscaped banks.

    Join the many cultural events organized along the riverbanks.

    Must-Do Activities on the Banks

    The Seine banks are not just about breathtaking views:
    they are also a hub for various activities for all ages. Culture lovers
    can explore floating museums and outdoor exhibitions. Music enthusiasts
    will enjoy the concerts and performances that regularly animate these spaces.
    If you're looking for a more relaxed vibe, opt for a Seine cruise.
    These boat trips offer a unique perspective on the city,
    showcasing its most famous monuments. The banks are also perfect for
    an impromptu picnic or a sunny reading session, away from the urban hustle.
